MAZE of UCSC
// SNAKE & LADDERS VARIANT — "MAZE TO SAVOR!"
A 3-player, 3-floor turn-based maze game built in C. Roll dice, ride stairs, slide poles, survive the Bawana zone, capture opponents — and race to plant your flag.
M.Y Hanan Mohamed University of Colombo School of Computing September 14, 2025
Core Systems
Dice Movement
Direction-aware movement with MP cost per cell. Full multi-step traversal per roll.
Dynamic Stairs
Direction flips every 5 rounds — UP / DOWN / BIDIRECTIONAL. Always changing the meta.
One-Way Poles
Slide down only. Higher priority than stairs when both occupy the same cell.
Bawana Zone
6 chaotic effects inside 12 special cells. Enter only at MP=0 — dangerous territory.
MP System
Capped at 250. Bonuses, penalties, and multipliers keep every turn unpredictable.
Capture & Reset
Land on an opponent — send them back to [0,6,12], direction NORTH. Brutal.

Design Decisions
FeatureLogic
Bawana entranceCells [0,9,19] — enterable only at MP=0. Forces direction NORTH on entry.
Starting area[0,6–9, 8–16]. Must roll a 6 to enter the maze.
Captured resetSent to [0,6,12], direction NORTH. MP preserved.
MP bonuses25% → +1–2 MP / 10% → +3–5 MP / 5% → 2× or 3× MP (capped at 250)
Flag validationBFS at startup. Unreachable flag replaced with a valid cell and logged.
Overlap priorityPole > Stair. Tie: Manhattan distance to flag. Deterministic — first wins.
Infinite loopsDetected within 100 steps. Reset to [0,6,12], MP preserved, logged.

Bawana Effects
Food Poisoning
Skip 3 turns → placed randomly inside Bawana → new effect applied.
Disoriented
Random direction each move for 4 turns. Good luck navigating.
Triggered
Move 2× the rolled amount for 4 turns. High risk, high reward.
Happy
Instant +200 MP. No lasting effect. Enjoy it while it lasts.
Random MP
Gain +10 to +100 MP per turn for 4 turns. Variable fuel boost.
MP = 0
Sent to a random Bawana interior cell. Another effect awaits.
